Common Questions

Can my clients see VoidPanel branding?

No. You set a custom brand name in your reseller settings. Clients see your brand on login and inside the panel.

What happens if a client exceeds their storage?

The client's account is blocked from creating new files at their package limit. The reseller pool protects the wider system.

Can I upgrade my pool later?

Yes — upgrade storage or account slots at any time from your reseller dashboard. Existing client accounts are never affected.

Is there a control panel for my clients?

Yes. Each client gets a full VoidPanel dashboard to manage their site, emails, databases, FTP, and backups.

What are the email and database limits per client?

Each client is assigned a Hosting Package (e.g., Starter, Pro) defining their email and DB limits. You choose the package at account creation.

How quickly can I create a client account?

Account provisioning takes under 30 seconds. DNS propagation may take a few minutes on first setup.
