Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need separate logins for each platform?
No — you connect all platforms from inside your VoidPanel dashboard using OAuth. One login, everything in one place.
Can I try before buying?
All plans activate instantly after payment with a 7-day satisfaction guarantee. Contact support if unsatisfied.
Will it work with my existing VoidPanel hosting?
Yes — Social Media Manager is a standalone add-on. It works regardless of your hosting plan.
Can I upgrade later?
Yes — upgrade from Starter to Pro or Agency at any time from the portal.
How many social accounts can I connect?
Depends on your plan — Starter supports 3 accounts, Pro supports 10, and Agency is unlimited across all 9 platforms.
