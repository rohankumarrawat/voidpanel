About Us

Built for Developers & Sysadmins

VoidPanel is a free, open-source web hosting control panel designed to give you complete, intuitive control over your Linux server — without the complexity or cost of legacy panels.

Our Mission

Server Management Should Be Free

We believe powerful server management tools shouldn't come with expensive monthly fees. VoidPanel was built to offer a modern, feature-rich alternative to cPanel — completely free and open source.

From DNS configuration and email hosting to SSL management and database tools, VoidPanel handles everything a web hosting server needs — with a clean, modern interface that feels premium without the price tag.
