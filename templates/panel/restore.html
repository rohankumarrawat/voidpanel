Understanding the migration flow
  • Local File: VoidPanel will analyze the uploaded configuration file structure and remap it to your system.
  • Panel Authentication: VoidPanel will attempt to connect via API/SSH, automatically trigger a platform backup, download it, and restore it into the assigned user's environment.
  • Process occurs in the background. A notification will appear in the dashboard upon completion.
